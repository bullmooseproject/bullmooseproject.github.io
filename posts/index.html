Writing Your Bucket List

When deciding to take control of your life, you have to figure out exactly what that means. Yeah sure, you want to do more that is interesting to you. But what do you actually want to do? That’s generally harder than you expect. This is where a bucket list comes in handy. To begin steering your life in the direction you want, you first must figure out what you want to do.
